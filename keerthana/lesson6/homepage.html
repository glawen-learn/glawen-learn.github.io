IRCTC

Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC) is a subsidiary of the Indian Railways that handles the catering, tourism and online ticketing operations of the Indian railways, with around 5,50,000 to 6,00,000 bookings every day is the world's second busiest and highest of 15 to 16 lakh tickets every day.[2] Its tagline is "Lifeline of the nation".
